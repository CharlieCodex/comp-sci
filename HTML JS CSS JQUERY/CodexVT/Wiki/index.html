Introduction

Codex Academy is ready for summer 2016. We are pleased to be back at Long Trail School's LTSummer camp facility with new curriculum and additions to our staff.

This year, we will offer Video Game Development for two one-week sessions, July 11-15 and July 18-22, 2016. Students who attended last year may want to attend the second week as we expect to have more experienced campers and more advanced instruction.
